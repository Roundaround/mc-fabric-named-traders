Naming wandering traders or their llamas prevents them from despawning.

It's as simple as that! In vanilla Minecraft, wandering traders and their llamas will despawn. Taming the llamas can keep them around, but name tags don't actually do anything, until now! Simply name that trader and their llamas to give yourself a permanent friend!

Pairs wonderfully with my [Babies Forever](https://modrinth.com/mod/roundaround-babies-forever) mod, which keeps all named children young forever! With the combination of these mods, you can build an army of baby trader llamas!
